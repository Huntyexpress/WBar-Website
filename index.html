What Is Chiropractic

Chiropractic is focused on the body’s ability to self-heal through chiropractic adjustments and various forms of muscle work. By improving the neuromusculoskeletal system’s ability to perform, we believe this improves the function of the systems throughout our body.

What To Expect When You Receive Care

New Patient

1.Paperwork (Bring Drivers License & Insurance Card)

2. Hot packs if desired

3. Adjustment, Muscle work, Cupping

(2 & 3 are same for existing patients)

Forms Of Payment

We are accepting a majority of insurances!


Cash, Check, Debit/Credit Card, HSA Cards


Cash patients, Co-payments, deductibles, and/or coinsurance payments are due at the time of service. Insurance companies require that we collect these fees because they are the terms of your health care contract. Ultimately, the patient is responsible for all balances.
